Money Management

Money Management is a tool that analyzes your monthly income and with out algorithm gives you suggestion on how you can invest your money to make more money, with the least amount of risk.
By organizing your monthly expenses on a month to month bases you can organize yourself and save and earn extra money.



Enter your monthly income after taxes




Enter your total cost for non negotiable liabilities(things you cannot change easily;rent,utilities bills,morgage)




Enter your total cost for negotiable liabilities(things you can easily change; food,memberships,phoneplans)




Enter amount spent on non essentails (clothes,furniture)




Enter amount saved
